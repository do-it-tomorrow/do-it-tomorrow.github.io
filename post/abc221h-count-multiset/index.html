所以对于 m→∞m\to \inftym→∞ 的情况，就直接：

fi,j=fi−1,j+fi,j−if_{i,j}=f_{i-1,j}+f_{i,j-i} fi,j​=fi−1,j​+fi,j−i​

fi,j=fi,j−i+∑k=1min⁡(m,i)gi−k,j,gi,j=fi,j−if_{i,j}=f_{i,j-i}+\sum\limits_{k=1}^{\min(m,i)}g_{i-k,j},g_{i,j}=f_{i,j-i} fi,j​=fi,j−i​+k=1∑min(m,i)​gi−k,j​,gi,j​=fi,j−i​
